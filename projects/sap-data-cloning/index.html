SAP Data Cloning & Integration Platform

A resilient synchronization platform that moves high-volume SAP data into internal reporting stores through orchestrated queues, batching, checkpoints, retries, and operational monitoring.

Business challenge

Reporting needed reliable SAP data without fragile manual pulls

Multiple internal applications required timely SAP data, while large historical and incremental loads needed to be controlled, observable, and recoverable when a network or processing failure occurred.

Solution

An orchestrated synchronization layer

The platform separates extraction, queue processing, checkpoint management, persistence, and monitoring. Workers process data in batches, track progress, resume from checkpoints, retry failed jobs, and expose status for operational support.

My contribution

From business discovery to production delivery.

  • Designed the orchestration flow for historical and incremental synchronization.
  • Implemented queue workers, batch processing, checkpoint persistence, retries, and progress monitoring.
  • Optimized database writes and reporting access for large enterprise datasets.
  • Managed Linux deployment, environment configuration, logs, and production troubleshooting.
