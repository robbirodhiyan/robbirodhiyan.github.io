Integrated HRIS & Workforce Workflow

An integrated workforce platform for e-leave, permissions, attendance, overtime, payroll preparation, approval hierarchies, and HR reporting across employee and management roles.

Open interactive demo Discuss this experience

Business challenge

Workforce processes were difficult to track end to end

Leave, permission, attendance, overtime, and approval activities required consistent rules, status visibility, and reliable records for employees, supervisors, and HR administrators.

Solution

A role-based digital HR workflow

The HRIS centralizes employee requests, multi-level approvals, attendance monitoring, overtime records, reporting, and payroll-readiness data in a single web application with clear audit trails.

My contribution

From business discovery to production delivery.

  • Mapped employee, supervisor, and HR workflows and approval rules.
  • Designed database structures, access control, transaction statuses, and reporting outputs.
  • Developed backend, frontend, notifications, validation, and administrative modules.
  • Supported UAT, user onboarding, deployment, maintenance, and enhancement requests.
