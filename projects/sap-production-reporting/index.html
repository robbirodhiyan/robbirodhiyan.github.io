SAP Production Reporting & OEE

A production intelligence platform that converts SAP-connected operational data into fast, traceable reporting for Yield, OEE, targets, shifts, output, and waste.

Business challenge

Slow, fragmented production reporting

Production teams spent significant time collecting, reconciling, and validating data before KPIs could be reviewed.

Solution

A single SAP-connected operational view

The system synchronizes production data, calculates KPIs, and presents dashboards, production-order details, filters, and exports.
