SAP Production Reporting & OEE

A production intelligence platform that converts SAP-connected operational data into fast, traceable reporting for Yield, OEE, target achievement, shift performance, output, and waste.

Business challenge

Slow, fragmented production reporting

Production teams depended on time-consuming data collection and reconciliation before performance reports could be reviewed. The delay reduced visibility into Yield, OEE, target-versus-actual performance, and operational losses.

Solution

A single SAP-connected operational view

The system synchronizes production data into an internal reporting layer, calculates key manufacturing metrics, and presents them through responsive dashboards, detailed production-order views, filters, and exportable reports.

My contribution

From business discovery to production delivery.

  • Led requirement gathering and process mapping with production stakeholders.
  • Designed the application architecture, data flow, access model, and reporting workflow.
  • Implemented Laravel backend services, SAP REST API integration, dashboards, and database optimization.
  • Coordinated UAT, deployment, production support, and continuous improvement.
