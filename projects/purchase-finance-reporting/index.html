SAP Purchase & Finance Reporting

A SAP-connected reporting platform that improves procurement and finance visibility through purchase-order monitoring, goods-receipt tracking, spend analysis, account traceability, and operational dashboards.

Business challenge

Procurement data was difficult to consolidate and analyze

Purchase orders, goods receipts, vendor transactions, materials, and financial posting information needed a clear reporting layer for monitoring, reconciliation, and faster business review.

Solution

A consolidated purchase and finance reporting layer

The application synchronizes SAP purchasing and accounting data, applies business mappings, and presents searchable reports, PO and GR status, vendor analysis, transaction details, and dashboard metrics for operational users.

My contribution

From business discovery to production delivery.

  • Gathered reporting requirements from purchasing and finance stakeholders.
  • Designed data mappings, report structures, filters, detail views, and integration processes.
  • Implemented SAP data synchronization, Laravel reporting, SQL optimization, and responsive dashboards.
  • Supported validation, reconciliation, UAT, deployment, and production issue analysis.

Key capabilities

  • Goods receipt tracking
