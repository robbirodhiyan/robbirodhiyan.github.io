FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Final answers may vary based on scope, system access, and project requirements.

Can you build an MVP first?

Yes. An MVP helps validate the core need before investing in a broader feature set.

Is the service suitable for small businesses or personal brands?

Yes. The scope can range from landing pages and catalogs to booking and transaction systems.

Can the mobile app support Android and iOS?

With React Native, one codebase can target Android and iOS with platform-specific adjustments where needed.

Can it integrate payments and WhatsApp?

Yes, using a sandbox or the client's official provider accounts and following the provider's terms.
