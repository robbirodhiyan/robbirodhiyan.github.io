FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Final answers may vary based on scope, system access, and project requirements.

Must all modules be built at once?

No. Implementation can begin with priority modules such as leave, attendance, inventory, or purchasing.

Can payroll be customized?

Yes, but formulas and sensitive data require strict validation and access controls.

Can it integrate fingerprint attendance devices?

Yes, when the device or system provides an officially accessible file, database, or API.

Is it suitable for small companies?

Yes. The scope and modules can match the organization's needs and growth stage.
