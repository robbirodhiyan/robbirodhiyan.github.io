FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Final answers may vary based on scope, system access, and project requirements.

Can you continue an existing application?

Yes, after reviewing the codebase, database, dependencies, security, and deployment process.

Is source code handed over?

Source-code ownership and handover are agreed in the project scope and contract.

Can you work with an internal team?

Yes. I can contribute as a technical lead, consultant, reviewer, or hands-on developer.

Is post-launch support available?

A stabilization period, maintenance, monitoring, and further development can be arranged.
