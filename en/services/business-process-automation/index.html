FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Final answers may vary based on scope, system access, and project requirements.

Must the process already be documented?

No. I can help discover the process through stakeholder discussions and observation of the current workflow.

Can it integrate existing systems?

Yes. The solution can be a new module, API integration, automation worker, or portal connecting multiple systems.

Can approvals differ by department?

Yes. Rules can depend on department, amount, transaction type, role, or other business parameters.

Is an audit trail available?

Yes. Status changes, approvers, timestamps, and notes can be recorded as required.
